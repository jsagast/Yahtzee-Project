Yahtzee is a dice game where the goal is to score the most points by rolling five dice to make specific combinations.
Each player takes turns rolling the dice up to three times per turn, choosing which dice to keep and which to reroll.
After rolling, players must choose a category to score, such as Ones, Twos, Threes, Fours, Fives, Sixes, Three of a Kind, Four of a Kind, Full House, Small Straight, Large Straight, Yahtzee, or Chance.
Points are calculated based on the category rules, and each category can only be used once per game.
A bonus of 35 points is awarded in the Upper Section if the total reaches 63 or more.
Rolling multiple Yahtzees after the first earns extra points (100) YOU HAVE TO SELECT ANOTHER CATEGORY IF THIS HAPPENS.
The game ends when all categories for all players are filled, and the player with the highest total score wins.
